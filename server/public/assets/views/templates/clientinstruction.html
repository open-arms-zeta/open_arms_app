Instructions

Welcome Page

On the Welcome Page, your Five (5) Default Meals are listed for your approval.

    If you are satisfied with the default selection for the week:

  • Click the "I Prefer The Default Options Listed Above" button
  • On the confirmation pop-up, click the "Confirm Meal Selection" button and you are DONE!

    If you would like to customize your meals for the week:

  • Click the "I Would Like To Customize Meals" button
  • You will be sent to the Customize Meal Selection Page

Customize Meal Selection Page

On the Customize Meal Selection Page you may customize meal options for the specified week

  • You may view meal options in each Category by clicking on the Category name
  • You may select specific meals simply by clicking the "Add Meal" button.
  • You may select up to five (5) meals for the week
  • Once you are satisfied with your meal selection for the week, click the "Submit Meal Selection" button.
  • On the confirmation pop-up, click the "Confirm Meal Selection button" and you are DONE!
